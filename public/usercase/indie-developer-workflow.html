配置代码示例Configuration Example

# OpenClaw 核心配置示例OpenClaw Core Configuration Example
agents:
  - name: MailPilot
    description: "智能邮件处理助手"
    skills: [gmail]
    triggers:
      - type: "cron"
        schedule: "*/15 * * * *"
    output_channel: "telegram_private"

  - name: CodeReviewer
    description: "GitHub 代码审查 Agent"
    skills: [github]
    triggers:
      - type: "github_webhook"
        event: "pull_request.opened"
    output_channel: "slack_dev_channel"

  - name: Scheduler
    skills: [gmail, calendar]
    triggers:
      - type: "email_parser"
        pattern: ".*会议邀请.*"
    output_channel: "telegram_private"

global_config:
  heartbeat_interval: 300
  log_level: INFO

实操建议Practical Recommendations

建议一Tip 1
从小处着手，逐步迭代Start Small, Iterate Gradually
从最频繁、最机械化的重复任务开始，例如邮件分类或简单的代码检查。Begin with the most frequent and mechanical repetitive tasks, such as email classification or simple code checks.
建议二Tip 2
善用多 Agent 隔离特性Leverage Multi-Agent Isolation
为不同功能创建独立 Agent，避免冲突，提高系统健壮性和可维护性。Create independent Agents for different functions to avoid conflicts and improve system robustness and maintainability.
建议三Tip 3
灵活运用触发器与渠道Use Triggers and Channels Flexibly
结合工作习惯选择触发机制（Cron、Webhook），将输出连接到常用通知渠道。Choose trigger mechanisms (Cron, Webhook) based on your workflow, and connect outputs to your preferred notification channels.
建议四Tip 4
拥抱开源，掌控数据Embrace Open Source, Own Your Data
选择本地化部署方案，完全掌控数据和工作流，保障隐私安全。Choose local deployment for complete control over your data and workflows, ensuring privacy and security.
建议五Tip 5
积极参与开源社区Actively Engage with the Open Source Community
在 GitHub 或 Discord 社区寻求帮助和分享经验，共同推动项目发展。Seek help and share experiences in the GitHub or Discord community to collectively advance the project.
