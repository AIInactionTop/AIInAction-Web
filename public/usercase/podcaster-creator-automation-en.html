Pain Points

Content Overload,
Energy Burnout

Solo creators wear every hat — director, editor, marketer, growth hacker. One person's energy can never keep up with multi-platform content demands.

#
Pain Point
Cost
01
Topic Ideation Burnout
Constant pressure to find fresh angles every week. Trend tracking, competitor analysis, audience interest mapping — topic research alone takes 3 hours. Ideation fatigue leads to declining content quality.
02
Post-Production Time Sink
Recording takes 1 hour, but editing, writing show notes, adding timestamps, and crafting descriptions takes 8 hours. Post-production consumes 80% of creative time.
03
Multi-Platform Distribution Nightmare
YouTube, Spotify, Apple Podcasts, Newsletter — each platform has different formats, SEO rules, and optimal posting times. Manual distribution takes 2 hours per episode.
04
Growth Plateau
Getting from 10K to 50K relies on content quality. Breaking past 50K requires growth strategy. But solo creators have no time for A/B testing, email marketing, or cross-promotions. Growth rate stuck at 3%.
05
Guest Management Chaos
Outreach emails, scheduling, prep materials, post-recording follow-up — managing one guest is like managing a small project. More guests means exponentially more admin burden.
06
Monetization vs Creation Tension
Sponsor outreach, ad integration, contract review — monetization admin devours creative time. Sacrificing content quality for revenue ultimately means losing both.

1. Research guest background, generate outline
2. Send technical check checklist
3. Confirm recording time and platform

Do NOT: Over-contact guests.
Max 2 follow-ups, 3 days apart.

Respect their time.
